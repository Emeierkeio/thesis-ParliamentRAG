Wire F4 (vote facts injected into the RAG generation prompt) and F1 (vote_coherence SSE event) into the query pipeline. Vote facts are computed in `query.py` (which holds the Neo4j client) and passed into `DirectWriter.generate`; vote coherence is emitted as a new SSE event for the chat UI (rendered later in 14-06). Output: modified `direct_writer.py`, modified `query.py` (two new SSE events), extended SSE contract test.
