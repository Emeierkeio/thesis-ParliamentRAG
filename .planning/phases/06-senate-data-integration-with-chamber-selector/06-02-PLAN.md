<objective>
Add chamber filtering to all backend retrieval channels and extend ChatRequest with a `chamber` field.

Purpose: When the frontend sends `chamber: "camera" | "senato" | "both"`, the backend must filter retrieval results to the selected chamber(s). All 3 retrieval channels (dense, sparse, graph) need `WHERE s.chamber IN $chambers` in their Cypher queries.
Output: Updated ChatRequest model, retrieval engine, and all 3 channel files with chamber filtering.
</objective>
